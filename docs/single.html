Role of GB in institutional investors portfolios

November 6, 2025 federico Nardoni

This study examines the role of Green Bonds within institutional investors’ portfolios, with the aim of assessing whether sustainable financial instruments alter the traditional principles of portfolio management. In particular, the analysis investigates the trade-off between profitability and risk, comparing portfolios composed of green bond ETFs with portfolios based on conventional corporate bonds. Using a quantitative framework grounded in modern portfolio theory, the study evaluates performance and resilience through risk-adjusted and downside risk measures, namely the Sharpe Ratio, Value at Risk (VaR), and Conditional Value at Risk (CVaR), in order to determine whether the integration of sustainability objectives can be achieved without compromising financial efficiency.
